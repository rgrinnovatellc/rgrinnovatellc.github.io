Conference Venues

SIGOPS

  1. HotOS: https://sigops.org/s/conferences/hotos/ -- happens in every two years
  2. SOSP: https://sigops.org/s/conferences/sosp/ -- happens every year

USENIX

  1. OSDI: https://www.usenix.org/conference/osdi25 -- happens every year
  2. NSDI: https://www.usenix.org/conference/nsdi25 -- happens every year
  3. ATC: https://www.usenix.org/conference/atc24 -- happens every year

Other venues

  1. ASPLOS: https://www.asplos-conference.org/ -- happens every year
  2. Eurosys: https://www.eurosys.org/ -- happens every year

SOSP generally holds the highest prestige due to its exclusive focus on operating systems principles, while ASPLOS and EuroSys have a broader scope encompassing system architecture and programming languages, with HotOS focusing more specifically on operating systems design and implementation.

Mobile

  • MobiHoc—Mobile Ad Hoc Networking and Computing—happens every year (https://www.sigmobile.org/mobihoc)
  • MOBICOM—Mobile Computing and Networking—happens every year (https://www.sigmobile.org/mobicom)
  • USENIX Security Symposium—happens every year (https://www.usenix.org/conference/usenixsecurity23)
  • MobiSys—International Conference on Mobile Systems—happens every year (https://www.sigmobile.org/mobisys/)
  • TMC—IEEE Transactions on Mobile Computing
  • MONET—Mobile Networks and Applications
  • MCCR—Mobile Computing and Communications Review
  • PerCom—Pervasive and Mobile Computing
  • HOTMOBILE—Pervasive and Mobile Computing
