RGR Innovate LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Earthquakes by RGR mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

What Our App Does

Earthquakes by RGR displays USGS earthquake data in an easy-to-read format. We use Google Places API for address search and Firebase Crashlytics for app stability.

Information We Collect
Location Data

When you search for locations, the app may access your device's location to provide relevant earthquake data for your area.

Address Searches

When you type addresses, this information is sent to Google Places API for address autocomplete suggestions. We do not store your search queries.

Crash Reports

Firebase Crashlytics automatically collects crash reports and basic device information to help us improve app stability. This includes:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version
  • Crash logs and error details
How We Use Your Information
  • Display earthquake data - Show relevant USGS information based on your location or search
  • Improve app performance - Use crash reports to fix bugs and enhance stability
  • Provide address suggestions - Use Google Places API to help you find locations
Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. We only share data with:

  • Google Places API - For address search functionality
  • Firebase Crashlytics - For crash reporting and app analytics
  • USGS - To fetch earthquake data (no personal information shared)
Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of crash reporting
  • Contact us with privacy concerns
Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app.
