Privacy

Privacy Policy

Data use, handling, and contact for Android applications developed by RGR Innovate LLC.

Last updated April 12, 2026

This policy applies to Android applications developed by RGR Innovate LLC. Specific data practices vary by app and are detailed below.

Contact Questions about privacy

Contact rgrinnovatellc@gmail.com for questions.

SmartConvert — Utility Toolkit

No Data Collection

SmartConvert does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app operates entirely offline on your device. There are no ads, no analytics, no crash reporting, and no third-party tracking services of any kind.

SmartConvert — Permissions

Camera and Storage

  • Camera: Used only when you choose to capture a photo for image conversion or use optional ARCore room measurement. Images and measurement data stay on your device and are not uploaded anywhere.
  • Storage/Files: Used only to read images you select and to save exported files to locations you choose.

SmartConvert — Processing

Local and Offline

  • All image processing, unit conversion, color conversion, and measurement calculations are performed locally on your device.
  • No files, images, or measurement data are transmitted over the network.
  • Temporary camera capture files are stored in the app's cache directory and are subject to standard Android cache management.
  • Exported files are saved only to the destination you choose.

Other Applications — Information used

What the app may access

  • Optional permissions and feature data: some apps may request access only when needed for their features, such as location for nearby results.
  • Basic device and app information: used for troubleshooting, compatibility, and support.
  • Usage information: limited anonymous data may be used to improve reliability, performance, and usability.

Other Applications — Use and retention

How information is handled

  • Provide app features such as information display, conversions, measurements, filtering, and other user-requested functions.
  • Improve stability, functionality, and troubleshooting.
  • Retain information only as long as needed to operate, maintain, or improve the applications and comply with legal obligations.
  • Do not sell personal information.

All Applications — Sharing and security

Limits and safeguards

  • Information may be shared only with your consent, to meet legal obligations, or to protect users, the service, or legal rights.
  • Reasonable technical and organizational steps are taken to reduce the risk of unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or loss.
  • None of our apps are intended for children under 13, and personal information is not knowingly collected from children under 13.

All Applications — Your choices

What you can do

  • Decline optional permissions through your device settings.
  • Ask questions about data handling.
  • Request clarification when this policy changes.
  • Review this page for the latest updates.
