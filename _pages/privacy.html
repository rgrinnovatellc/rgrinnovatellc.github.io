SmartConvert

Information Collection

SmartConvert does not automatically collect, store, transmit, or otherwise disclose personal information. The application operates locally on your device and does not include advertising, analytics, crash reporting, or third-party tracking services.

SmartConvert — Permissions

Permissions and Device Access

  • Camera: Access is used only when you choose to capture a photo for image conversion or use optional ARCore room measurement. Images and measurement data remain on your device and are not uploaded for processing.
  • Storage/Files: Access is used only to read images you select and to save exported files to locations you choose.

SmartConvert — Local Processing

Local Processing and Storage

  • Image processing, unit conversion, color conversion, and measurement calculations are performed locally on your device.
  • Files, images, and measurement data are not transmitted over the network for processing.
  • Temporary camera capture files may be stored in the app cache directory and remain subject to standard Android cache management.
  • Exported files are saved only to the destination you choose.
