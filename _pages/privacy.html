Privacy

Privacy Policy

Data use, handling, and contact for Earthquakes by RGR.

Last updated March 11, 2026

This policy applies to Earthquakes by RGR, an Android application that provides earthquake information using public data sources and optional location-based filtering.

Contact Questions about privacy

Contact rgrinnovatellc@gmail.com for questions.

Information used

What the app may access

  • Location data: only if you grant permission, so the app can show nearby earthquake information or distance-based filters.
  • Basic device and app information: used for troubleshooting and compatibility support.
  • Usage information: limited anonymous data that may help improve reliability and usability.

Use and retention

How information is handled

  • Show earthquake information relevant to your selected or current location.
  • Improve stability, functionality, and troubleshooting.
  • Retain information only as long as needed to operate, maintain, or improve the application and comply with legal obligations.
  • Do not sell personal information.

Sharing and security

Limits and safeguards

  • Information may be shared only with your consent, to meet legal obligations, or to protect users, the service, or legal rights.
  • Reasonable technical and organizational steps are taken to reduce the risk of unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or loss.
  • The app is not intended for children under 13, and personal information is not knowingly collected from children under 13.

Your choices

What you can do

  • Decline location access through your device settings.
  • Ask questions about data handling.
  • Request clarification when this policy changes.
  • Review this page for the latest updates.
