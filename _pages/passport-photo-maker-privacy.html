Privacy

Passport Photo Maker Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Passport Photo Maker handles information when you use the application.

Back to privacy overview

Last updated April 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies specifically to Passport Photo Maker, a paid, ad-free Android application published by RGR Innovate LLC.

Contact Privacy inquiries

For privacy questions or app support requests, contact rgrinnovatellc@gmail.com.

Passport Photo Maker

Information Collection

Passport Photo Maker does not automatically collect, store, transmit, or otherwise disclose personal information from within the application. Photo processing occurs locally on your device. The application does not include analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs.

Passport Photo Maker — Permissions

Permissions and Device Access

  • Camera: Access is used only when you choose to capture a photo for processing.
  • Storage/Files: Access is used only to read images you select and to save processed photos or print sheets to locations you choose.

Passport Photo Maker — Local Processing

Local Processing and Storage

  • Face detection, selfie segmentation, background removal, cropping, compliance checks, and image enhancement are performed locally on your device.
  • Photos, image content, and face data are not transmitted over the network for processing.
  • Temporary files may be created within the app cache directory and remain subject to standard Android cache management.
  • Processed photos are stored only where you choose to save them.

Passport Photo Maker — Purchases

Purchases and External Services

  • Passport Photo Maker is distributed and sold through Google Play.
  • Google Play may process purchase, billing, tax, and refund information in accordance with its own policies.
  • RGR Innovate LLC does not receive or store your full payment card details through the application.
  • ML Kit face detection and selfie segmentation operate on-device with bundled models.

Passport Photo Maker — Support

Support Communications

  • If you contact support by email, RGR Innovate LLC receives only the information you choose to provide, which may include your email address, message content, screenshots, device details, and purchase information.
  • That information is used solely to respond to your request and provide support.
  • Support communications are retained only as long as reasonably necessary to address your request.

Passport Photo Maker — Summary

Summary of Practices

  • The application is paid and ad-free.
  • Photo processing occurs locally on your device.
  • No in-app analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs are used.
  • Purchases and refunds are handled through Google Play.
